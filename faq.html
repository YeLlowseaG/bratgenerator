🎯 General Questions

Brat Generator is a free online tool that lets you create custom album covers inspired by Charli XCX's iconic "Brat" album aesthetic. You can type any text, choose green or white backgrounds, add effects, and download instantly.

Yes! Brat Generator is completely free forever. You can create unlimited covers, download them in high quality, and use them for personal or commercial purposes. No hidden fees, no subscriptions.

🎨 How to Use

  1. Type your text in the input field
  2. Choose between Green Mode or White Mode
  3. Optionally toggle Speed Lines effect
  4. Click "Download PNG" to save your cover

Green Mode creates covers with the iconic lime green background (#8ace00), just like the original Brat album. White Mode creates covers with a white background and green text, offering a clean alternative aesthetic.

Speed Lines are an optional visual effect that adds radiating lines around your text, creating a dynamic, energetic feel. Perfect for adding extra flair to your brat covers!

📱 Gallery & Downloads

Yes! Each cover in our Gallery has a download button. Simply hover over any gallery item and click the 📥 download icon to get that cover as a high-quality PNG file (800x800 pixels).

All covers are downloaded as PNG files with transparent backgrounds where applicable. The main generator creates 800x800 pixel images, perfect for album covers, social media, and printing.

Absolutely! While the interface is available in 7 languages, you can type your cover text in any language or script. The tool automatically handles text wrapping and sizing for all languages, including non-Latin scripts.

⚙️ Technical & Troubleshooting

Brat Generator automatically adjusts font size to fit your text. For very long text, try shorter phrases or use line breaks. The tool works best with 1-4 words for the most authentic brat aesthetic.

Make sure your browser allows downloads from our site. Try disabling popup blockers temporarily. If the issue persists, try right-clicking the download button and selecting "Save link as" or contact us for support.
