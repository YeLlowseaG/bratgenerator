About Brat Generator

Born from Brat Summer 2024

When Charli XCX dropped "Brat" in June 2024, it wasn't just an album—it was a cultural moment. The iconic lime green cover with its bold, lowercase typography became the defining aesthetic of summer 2024.

Brat Generator was created to let everyone participate in this cultural phenomenon. Whether you're making memes, expressing yourself, or just having fun with the aesthetic, our tool makes it simple to create your own brat-style covers.
